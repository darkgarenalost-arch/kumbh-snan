About Ganga Jal

Sacred water, collected with devotion

Ganga Jal holds a place of deep reverence in Hindu tradition, used to mark life's most sacred moments — from the first breath of a newborn to daily worship at the home mandir.

Our bottles are collected from the Triveni Sangam in Nashik, the holy confluence that hosts the Kumbh Mela, and sealed the same day to preserve their sanctity for your rituals.
