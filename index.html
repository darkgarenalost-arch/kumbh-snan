Kumbh Mela is the world's largest spiritual gathering — a divine opportunity for purification that comes once every 12 years. But for millions of devotees — elderly, unwell, NRIs, or bound by circumstance — attending physically is simply not possible.

We founded Kumbh Snan with one belief: distance should never separate a devotee from God's grace. Our trained Vedic pandits perform authentic Snans at the holy Nashik ghats so your blessings are real and witnessed.
