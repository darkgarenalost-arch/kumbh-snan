Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use Kumbh Snan's services.

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kumbh Snan ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website at kumbh-snan.com and our digital sacred ritual services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

When you book a service, contact us, or browse our website, we may collect the following categories of personal information:

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring URL (the page you visited before ours)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Click behaviour and scroll depth

This information is collected through the tools described in Section 3 below and is used to improve our website and understand how visitors find and use our services.

1.3 WhatsApp Communications

We use WhatsApp as our primary communication channel to deliver your video proof, confirm booking details, and provide customer support. Messages exchanged via WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy (Meta). We retain records of WhatsApp communications for service fulfilment, quality assurance, and customer support purposes.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect strictly for the following purposes:

  • Service Fulfilment: To perform your booked Kumbh Snan ritual, chanting your name and gotra at the holy ghat as you have instructed.
  • Booking Confirmation: To confirm your booking slot, package selection, and payment details via WhatsApp and email.
  • Video Proof Delivery: To deliver your personalised ritual video to your WhatsApp number within 24 hours of the ritual.
  • Customer Communication: To respond to your enquiries, provide support, and keep you informed about your booking status.
  • Payment Processing: To complete your payment transaction securely through Razorpay.
  • Service Improvement: To analyse website usage patterns (anonymised) and improve our offerings and user experience.
  • Marketing Communication: Occasionally to inform you of upcoming Kumbh Mela dates, new service offerings, or special announcements — only if you have not opted out.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations, including financial record-keeping obligations.

🔒 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their own marketing purposes. Period.

3 Third-Party Services We Use

To operate our website and deliver our services effectively, we use the following trusted third-party tools. Each of these has its own privacy policy and data practices:

3.1 Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel)

We use Meta Pixel on our website to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram. Meta Pixel collects information about visitor actions on our website (such as page views and clicks) and may set cookies in your browser. This data is processed by Meta Platforms Inc. and used to show you relevant advertisements. You can opt out of Meta's data tracking by visiting your Facebook Ad Preferences or using browser tools to block tracking cookies.

Meta's Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

3.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website — which pages are most visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. Google Analytics collects anonymised data and uses cookies. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Google Sheets (Booking Records)

Your booking information (name, phone, email, selected package, and payment ID) is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only by authorised Kumbh Snan team members. This data is used solely for managing bookings, communicating with you, and performing the ordered ritual. Google processes and stores this data on its secure servers.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.4 Razorpay (Payment Processing)

All payments on our website are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited, India's leading payment gateway. When you complete a payment, your financial information (card details, UPI, net banking credentials) is transmitted directly to Razorpay's encrypted, PCI-DSS compliant servers. Kumbh Snan does not store your full payment card details. Razorpay provides us with a transaction ID and payment confirmation, which we store alongside your booking record.

Razorpay's Privacy Policy: https://razorpay.com/privacy/

4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to improve your experience and collect analytics data. The cookies we use include:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used for basic website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Set by Google Analytics to track anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with our site.
  • Advertising Cookies: Set by Meta Pixel to track visitor actions for the purpose of targeted advertising on Facebook and Instagram platforms.
  • Payment Cookies: Set by Razorpay during the checkout process to enable secure payment functionality.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

5 Data Storage and Security

We take the protection of your personal information seriously. Your data is stored and protected as follows:

  • Booking records are stored in a private, password-protected Google Sheet accessible only to authorised team members.
  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by Razorpay using industry-standard PCI-DSS encryption. We do not store full card numbers or financial credentials.
  • WhatsApp communications are protected by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption.
  • We restrict access to personal data to only those team members who require it to fulfil your booking and deliver the service.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data, but we are committed to using commercially reasonable means to protect it.

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Booking records are retained for a minimum of 3 years to comply with Indian financial and tax record-keeping requirements.
  • Photographs submitted for ritual purposes are deleted within 90 days after the ritual has been performed and the video has been delivered.
  • WhatsApp communications are retained for up to 1 year for customer support and service quality purposes, after which they may be deleted or anonymised.
  • Analytics data collected via Google Analytics and Meta Pixel is retained per those platforms' own retention policies (typically 26 months for Google Analytics).

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

7 Your Rights

As a user of our services, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Right to Opt Out: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying "STOP" to any of our messages or emailing us.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 8. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
